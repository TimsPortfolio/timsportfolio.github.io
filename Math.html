Favorite Calculator: Desmos Graphing Calculator

    Pros
  • Contains Many Functions.
  • Autmoatically Graphs Equations.
  • Variables can be set to change numbers (slowly increase/decrease) allowing your graphs to move.
  • There are folders to organize all your functions. This is useful if you are making a drawing with math and while doing math to sort your equations by question number.
  • You can create an account to save all your graphs.
  • There are mobile apps so you can graph and calculate on the go.
    Cons
  • Saved graphs just appear in one long list, so for me it can be a little cluttered.

Overall this is my favorite calculator for school and fun stuff like this. I would highly recommend this calculator to everyone.
